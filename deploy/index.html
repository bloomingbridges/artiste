Gir loves tacos (2012)

Interactive animation on canvas

l'artiste is a minimal, (almost) production-ready template for HTML5 canvas development. It features an aspect-ratio agnostic, vertically-ruled and centred layout and comes with a bunch of helper libraries (mostly from the CreateJS family) right out of the box. Happy canvassing!
Includes EaselJS 0.4.2, TweenJS 0.2, SoundJS 0.2, AtelierJS 0.1 and PxLoader 0.1.

Special thanks go to Grant Skinner (@gskinner), Justin Windle (@soulwire), Alexis Sellier (@cloudhead), Robert Bringhurst and of course Jhonen Vasquez.
